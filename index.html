Pictographic Languges: An Exploration of Inspiration and Evolution

5/6 8-9 pm EST by Naomi

Breakout room: Chinese 001 with Naomi
The Chinese writing system is made up of pictographs and ideographs. With one associated word per character, this system is very complex and relies on memory, but there are patterns that connect various characters. Come see how the ancient Chinese people derived characters from the world around them and how they bring meaning in the writing system.
